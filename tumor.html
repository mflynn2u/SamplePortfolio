Tumor volume change for medications


This projects loads two csv data files and merges them to analize the efficiency of different medications.

Several comaprisons where calculated and graphed over time, including Metastatic sites, tumor volume, mortality ( mice in study ) and changes in tumor volume.

This project was made with : Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, numpy)
Data : csv
Tools: Jupyter notebook, Git.
